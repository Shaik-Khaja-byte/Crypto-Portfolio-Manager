MATHEMATICAL LOGIC DOCUMENTATION

1. Profit and Loss (PnL)
   The simplest way to measure performance.
   
   Formula:
   PnL = (Current Price - Buy Price) * Quantity

   Example:
   You bought 2 ETH at $2,000 each.
   Current Price of ETH is $3,000.
   
   PnL = ($3,000 - $2,000) * 2
       = $1,000 * 2
       = $2,000 Profit

2. Percentage Change
   Shows how much the asset has grown or shrunk relative to the initial investment.

   Formula:
   % Change = ((Current Price - Buy Price) / Buy Price) * 100

   Example (using above numbers):
   % Change = (($3,000 - $2,000) / $2,000) * 100
            = ($1,000 / $2,000) * 100
            = 0.5 * 100
            = 50% Increase

3. Portfolio Value Calculation
   The total value of all assets combined.

   Formula:
   Total Value = Sum of (Current Price of Coin A * Quantity of Coin A) + (Current Price of Coin B * Quantity of Coin B) + ...

   Example:
   Portfolio:
   - 2 ETH at $3,000
   - 1 BTC at $50,000

   Total Value = ($3,000 * 2) + ($50,000 * 1)
               = $6,000 + $50,000
               = $56,000
