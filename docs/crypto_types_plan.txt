CRYPTO TYPES PLANNING

1. Supported Crypto Assets
   We aim to support a diverse range of assets to reflect a realistic portfolio.

   a) Layer-1 Blockchains (The Foundation)
      - Bitcoin (BTC): The store of value.
      - Ethereum (ETH): The smart contract platform.
      - Solana (SOL): High-speed blockchain.
   
   b) Stablecoins (Risk Management)
      - USDT (Tether)
      - USDC (USD Coin)
      - Purpose: Used by traders to hedge against volatility. We must track these to show "cash on hand" positions.

   c) DeFi Tokens (Financial Applications)
      - Uniswap (UNI)
      - Aave (AAVE)
   
   d) Meme Coins (High Risk/Reward)
      - Dogecoin (DOGE)
      - Shiba Inu (SHIB)
      - Purpose: High volatility assets that users often track for fun or speculation.

2. Why CoinGecko?
   - Coverage: They track thousands of coins, including very small/new ones.
   - Simplicity: Their API is easy to use and doesn't require complex authentication for basic tiers.
   - Metadata: They provide good categorization (categories, tags) which helps us group coins later.

3. Future Scalability Ideas
   - Categorization: Automatically tag coins as "DeFi", "L1", etc. based on API metadata.
   - Multi-chain tracking: Tracking the *same* token across different networks (e.g., USDT on Ethereum vs. Tron).
   - NFT Support: Future modules could track floor prices of NFT collections.
